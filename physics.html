Electricity

Electric current is the rate of flow of electric charge through a conductor.

Important Units

  • Current → Ampere (A)
  • Voltage → Volt (V)
  • Resistance → Ohm (Ω)

Important Formula

Ohm's Law: V = IR

Sources of Energy

Sources of energy can be renewable or non-renewable.

  • Solar Energy
  • Wind Energy
  • Hydroelectric Energy
  • Fossil Fuels
