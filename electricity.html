⚡ Electricity

What is Electric Current?

Electric current is the rate of flow of electric charge through a conductor.

Important Units

  • Current → Ampere (A)
  • Voltage → Volt (V)
  • Resistance → Ohm (Ω)

Ohm's Law

The current flowing through a conductor is directly proportional to the voltage across it.

Formula:

V = IR

Applications

  • Electric circuits
  • Household wiring
  • Electronic devices
